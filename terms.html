Wilson Appliance Service Terms

Please review these terms before submitting a service request.

Service calls not covered by manufacturer warranty

  • We will send a technician to perform an on-site diagnostic of your appliance. The diagnostic fee is $169.95 (after tax) for the first appliance and $92.01 for each additional appliance. This diagnostic fee only applies if you choose not to proceed with the repair after receiving a quote.
  • If you are located outside of our service area, we recommend scheduling service directly with your manufacturer’s factory service provider. Most brands offer online scheduling.
  • The technician will diagnose the problem and prepare a quote for the parts and labor required to complete the repair.
  • You may either approve the quoted repair or decline further service. If you decline further service, only the diagnostic fee will be charged.
  • Quotes are valid for 14 days. If we do not receive approval within 14 days, we will collect the diagnostic fee and close the ticket under the assumption that you do not wish to proceed.
  • If you choose not to repair the unit and instead replace your appliance through Wilson’s, you will receive a $100 discount on your sales ticket when spending over $1,000 to help offset the cost of the diagnostic trip.
  • If you approve the repair quote, we will order the required parts.
  • Once parts arrive, we will contact you to schedule the return visit for installation.
  • After parts are installed and the repair is complete, you will be charged the repair amount you previously approved.

If the issue persists after service completion, we will provide one no-charge recall trip within 30 days of the initial repair completion so we can confirm the issue is fully resolved.

Service calls covered by manufacturer warranty

  • Functional issues caused by defects in materials or workmanship are generally covered by the manufacturer warranty.
  • Warranty coverage generally does not include cleaning, maintenance, customer education, incorrect application, installation issues, misuse, or cosmetic concerns outside the applicable cosmetic warranty period.
  • For most appliances, cosmetic warranty coverage is limited to the first 30 days after delivery.
  • Warranty terms vary by manufacturer and model. You must refer to your product’s user guide and manufacturer warranty documentation for exact coverage details.
The warranty statements above are general guidelines and are not absolute. Final warranty determination is subject to the manufacturer’s terms and diagnosis.
Wilson AC & Appliance reserves the right to update these terms at any time.
